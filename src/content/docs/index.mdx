## About This Book

**Author:** Miko (compiled with AI research assistance)
**Date:** February 2026
**Type:** Meta-analysis review

This book synthesises the current state of scientific knowledge on psoriasis, drawing on peer-reviewed literature spanning immunology, genetics, epidemiology, and therapeutics. It presents the molecular and cellular mechanisms of disease pathogenesis — including the central IL-23/IL-17 axis — in terms accessible to readers without prior immunology training.
